# Pre-planning

I want to create an events calendar app that:
- allows users to list national & religious holidays of their country,
- create and manage their own events (with categories, reminders, etc),
- Synchronize them with nextcloud and Google Calendar.

## Features

### Events

- Create and manage events (with categories, reminders, etc)
- Synchronize events with nextcloud and Google Calendar

### Holidays

- List national & religious holidays of the user's country
- Synchronize holidays with nextcloud and Google Calendar
